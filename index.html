About Us

Designed by developers for developers.

Devkitty is a lightweight JavaScript icon system designed to keep your markup clean and your projects fast. Instead of injecting raw SVG into the DOM, Devkitty renders icons from a compact file format, giving you full control over performance, loading behavior, and scalability. Whether you're building a small site or a large application with hundreds of icons, Devkitty ensures consistent rendering, strict structure, and zero unnecessary overhead.

Why Devkitty?

Devkitty delivers icons through a secure file (.GGDK) to your website. Providing performance, protection, and total control without polluting your DOM and long site loading times. We are supported by all modern web browsers such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge and OperaGX.

Protected by Design

Icons never appear as raw SVG in the DOM. They're reconstructed at runtime and applied via HTML5 Canvas, keeping your assets clean and protected.

Ultra-Light GGDK Format

A compact, structured file format built specifically for icons. Fast to parse, strict by design, and optimized for packages with 100+ icons.

Performance Loading

Load icons immediately, on idle, on hover, in viewport, or manually. Devkitty gives you full control over when rendering happens.
